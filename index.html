Hassang Account Deletion Request

If you would like to delete your Hassang account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to: hassang.team@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email address in the message

What data will be deleted:

  • Your account information (email, username)
  • Your workout data (plank, running stats)
  • Your leaderboard data

Data retention:

  • Your data will be permanently deleted within 7 days of request.

If you have any questions, contact us at: hassang.team@gmail.com
